Types of Customs Clearance Services:

Import clearance:

  • Import of goods from outside the country
  • Proper classification of goods based on HS codes.
  • Coordination with customs authorities to avoid delays.

Export clearance:

  • Ensuring compliance with all export documentation and procedures
  • Verification of shipments for restricted or controlled goods.
  • Coordination with port and airport authorities for smooth dispatch.

Goods We Handle:

Perishable Goods: Quick and temperature-controlled clearance.
Hazardous Goods: Compliance with safety standards and regulations.
Machinery and Equipment: Assistance with valuation and duty assessments.
General Merchandise: Efficient handling of a diverse range of goods.

Taxes, Duties, and Tariffs

Exemptions and Concessions: Guiding clients on eligibility for exemptions or reduced tariffs.

GST/IGST Handling: Ensuring correct filing and payment of applicable taxes.
